Welcome to YouSense!


In one sentence, the idea of YouSense is to collect experiences and feelings of people in different spaces in real-time. We believe that people's experience could support better understanding of the situation and even learning new things about spaces.
YouSense application runs in a Web browser both on computers and mobile devices. We are now developing visualizations of gathered experience data combined with daba about indoor climate conditions (temperature, humidity, energy levels, etc.).Â 
Currently, YouSense is running in TUAS building in Aalto campus in Otaniemi. We are now arranging trials in several other buildings both in and outside Aalto. If you are interested, please contact us.
